\section{2024 Workshop on Uncertainty Visualization}
{\em 2024 IEEE Uncertainty Visualization Workshop} chaired by Dr.~Athawale, Dr.~Johnson, Dr.~Pugmire, Dr.~Potter, and Dr.~Rosen was a big success. The workshop took place virtually\footnote{\tiny\url{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HjW__fdmpZo}} with more than $100$ viewers on the day of the workshop. The workshop was responded by renowned speakers and panelists in the fields of data and uncertainty visualization, including Dr.~Daniel Weiskopf, Dr.~Jessica Hullman, Dr.~Han-Wei Shen, Dr.~Gerik Scheuermann, Dr.~Lace Padilla, and Dr.~Matthew Kay, which led to stimulating interactions and identification of critical uncertainty visualization challenges. The workshop received $12$ paper submissions ($5$ long papers, $6$ short papers, and $1$ poster) that were co-authored by $42$ researchers. Submissions touched on important topics related to uncertainty, including uncertainty visualization techniques for diverse types of data (scalar, vector, multivariate), software (python tools), uncertainty communication (text and speech augmented with visualization to convey uncertainty), AI, and user studies. The papers were reviewed by a program committee of $21$ visualization researchers\footnote{\tiny\url{https://tusharathawale.github.io/UncertaintyVis-Workshop/organization.html}}.
