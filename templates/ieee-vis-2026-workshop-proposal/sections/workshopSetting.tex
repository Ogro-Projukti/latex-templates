\section{Workshop Settings and Expected Attendance}

We intend this workshop to be in-person. However, the workshop will include presented material and a panel (or keynote as a backup), all of which fit into physical settings or virtual environments without significant changes. Each attendee will need to have access to a laptop/desktop machine, irrespective of the conference setting. We expect around 30-40 participants. We expect our workshop to attract an audience with diverse expertise, including mathematics, high-performance computing, domain experts, and psychology, who may not have prior visualization experience. In the case of the need for a virtual setting, the presented materials and panel discussions will be shared via video conferencing, such as Zoom or MS Teams.
